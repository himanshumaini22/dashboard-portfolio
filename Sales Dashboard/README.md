**Business Objectives**
The primary goals of this dashboard are:
1. Track Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales performance.
2. Monitor sales trends over months and weeks.
3. Identify top performing products.
4. Analyze product category contribution to total sales.
5. Track customer engagement via reviews.
This enables businesses to improve inventory planning, marketing strategy, and sales forecasting.

**Key Metrics Displayed**

1️ **Performance KPIs**
- YTD Sales: Total revenue generated in the current year
- QTD Sales: Revenue generated in the selected quarter
- YTS Products Sold: Total number of products sold
- YTD Reviews: Total customer reviews received
These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business health.

2️⃣ **Sales Trend Analysis**
- Sales by Month
- Displays revenue trends across months
- Helps identify seasonal demand patterns
- Sales by Week
- Highlights short-term fluctuations in sales
- Useful for identifying promotional impacts or sudden demand spikes

3️⃣ **Product Category Analysis**
- Products Sold by Category
- Visualizes distribution of sales volume across product categories
- Helps identify dominant product segments
- **Example categories include:** Men Shoes, Men Clothes, Toys, Camera, Car Accessories, Mobile Accessories

4️⃣ **Product Performance**
- Top 5 Products by YTD Sales
- Identifies highest revenue generating products
- Top 5 Products by YTD Reviews
- Shows products receiving the highest customer engagement
This comparison helps identify products that are both high selling and highly rated.


**Dashboard Features**
- Quarter based filtering (Q1–Q4)
- Product category filtering
- Interactive visualizations
- KPI summary cards
- Trend analysis charts
- Product ranking visuals
These features allow users to drill down into data quickly and intuitively.
